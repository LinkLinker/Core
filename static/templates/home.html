    Het verhaal van Gassan

    Gassan, already one of the world’s leading diamond polishing and luxury jewelry and watch companies wants to strengthen its position in order to be not only the leading diamond polishing company in the Netherlands, but eventually, also in the whole world.

    Gassan will achieve her goals by offering craftsmanship creativity and customer care and by being the customers truly reliable companion in the dazzling world of diamonds, jewelry and watches.

    Gassan loves to share the values and passion for the fascinating diamond trade and aims to keep the diamond polishing craftsmanship alive for ever, like the diamonds themselves, who are and always will be ‘forever’.
